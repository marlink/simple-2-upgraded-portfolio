Responsive Behavior

All typography scales fluidly across breakpoints. Resize your browser window to see the typography adapt in real-time.

The system uses clamp() functions to ensure smooth scaling between:

  • XS: 300px
  • SM: 600px
  • MD: 768px
  • LG: 1024px
  • XL: 1280px
  • XXL: 1600px
  • XXXL: 2400px

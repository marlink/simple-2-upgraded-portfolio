The Core Problem: Power Without Process

  • Transparent decision log with rationale (Google Doc of Truth)
  • Weekly triage where execs publicly pick what *drops* if something new enters

Symptoms:

Fix:

  • Data-minimization checklist baked into design system
  • Threat-model & risk-acceptance doc attached to every epic

The Playbook: 8 Steps We Actually Follow

  2. Design principles as guardrails ‚Äì Co-write 5 bullets; anything that violates them needs an exception log.
  3. Research gates ‚Äì Discovery deliverables timeboxed; no spec without validation plan.
  4. Parallel approvals ‚Äì Brand, legal, eng review in same 48 h window; CEO attends strategic checkpoints only.
  5. Metrics over moods ‚Äì Translate ‚Äúfeels off‚Äù into measurable hypotheses (e.g., reduce drop-off 15%).
  6. Decision log ‚Äì Google Doc of Truth: request, data, trade-off, owner, date.
  7. Escalation path ‚Äì Single liaison (senior PM or design lead) fields ad-hoc asks; urgent items go through triage.
  8. Retros on process, not people ‚Äì Monthly health check: lead time, rework %, usability score, consistency index.

The sidebar automatically becomes sticky when scrolling and collapses to a single column on mobile devices. Available layout variants:

  • .sidebar-layout - Standard 280px left sidebar
  • .sidebar-layout--narrow - 240px sidebar
  • .sidebar-layout--wide - 320px sidebar
  • .sidebar-layout--right - Right-aligned sidebar

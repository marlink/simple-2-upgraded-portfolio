Usage guidelines

Keep imagery consistent across the brand by following these baseline rules. Each guideline maps to tokens in `framework-unified.css`.

Aspect ratios

Hero images scale to 16:9. Use utility classes `.ratio-16-9` or `.ratio-4-3` to maintain the crop without layout shift.

Lazy loading

Add `loading="lazy"` to non-critical imagery. Combine with `.skeleton` utility for perceived performance.

Colour grading

Light theme leans towards warm highlights. Dark theme requires cooler shadows to retain contrast.
